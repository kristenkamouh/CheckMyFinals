Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

University Exam Scheduler is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or exam schedule data to external servers.

  • All file processing happens locally in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers or third parties
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

2. How Your Data is Processed

When you upload a schedule file (.csv or .xlsx):

  • The file is processed entirely within your web browser
  • Data is stored temporarily in your browser's memory
  • No permanent storage occurs on your device
  • Refreshing or closing the page clears all data

3. Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party libraries:

  • SheetJS (xlsx.js) - For parsing Excel files, loaded from CDN
  • Google Fonts - For typography (Inter font family)

These services may collect minimal usage data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally in your browser and no data is transmitted or stored on our servers, your exam schedule information remains completely private and secure on your device.

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can clear all data by refreshing or closing the page
  • No account creation or registration is required
  • No data retention or history is maintained

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Help page.
